## Authenticating Gemstones: Genuine vs. Fake

Fake gemstones are rampant and devastate astrological results. Learning basic authentication saves money and ensures actual planetary benefit.

### Visual Authentication Tests (At Home)

| Test | Genuine Gems | Fake/Synthetic Gems |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Transparency** | Clear, lucid, light passes through naturally | Cloudy, opaque, or artificially bright |
| **Color saturation** | Rich, deep, but natural-looking | Overly vibrant, uniform (unnatural) |
| **Inclusions** | Tiny natural imperfections visible under magnification | Completely "perfect" (impossible in nature) |
| **Weight** | Heavy for size (high specific gravity) | Suspiciously light (synthetic/glass) |
| **Temperature** | Stays cool when held (gems retain coolness) | Warms quickly like plastic |
| **Hardness** | Cannot scratch easily (test on glass gently) | Scratches easily (synthetic) |
| **Refractive index** | Double refraction visible with light | Single refraction or none |

### Professional Authentication (Recommended)

For expensive purchases (ruby, diamond, emerald):
- Get certifications from reputed labs (GÜBELIN, GIA, IGI)
- Request thermal or UV tests
- Buy only from suppliers with documented provenance
- Avoid online "bargains" without authentication
- Invest in genuine stones; synthetics waste your effort and money

### Red Flags for Fake Gems

- Price too low compared to market rates
- Seller cannot provide certification
- "Treated" without transparent disclosure
- "Ethically sourced" but no verifiable history
- Seller avoids detailed specifications
- High-pressure sales tactics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take gemstones to show results?**

A: Results vary by gem, planetary position, and individual constitution. Generally: Emerald and red coral show fastest results (7–15 days for intellect/courage shift). Ruby and diamond take moderate time (15–40 days for confidence/beauty). Yellow sapphire and blue sapphire take longer (40–90 days for deep transformation). The key is wearing consistently during the initial 40 days; results deepen significantly by day 90. If wearing a gem for a weak planet (not just amplifying), the timeline extends to months or years as the planet gradually strengthens.

**Q: Can I wear multiple gemstones from different planets together?**

A: Yes, but with strategy. Complementary planets (like Jupiter and Mercury, or Venus and Saturn) combine powerfully. Conflicting planets (like Mars and Saturn, or Mercury and Ketu) can neutralize each other if worn simultaneously. Start with one gem, add a second after 40–90 days, and limit to 2–3 maximum. Our recommendation specifies which gems work together. Avoid the temptation to wear all nine Navratna at once; this creates confusion rather than clarity.

**Q: What if I can't afford a natural gemstone? Are treated or lab-created stones effective?**

A: Treated gemstones (heated natural gems) retain 80–95% of planetary potency and are much more affordable. Lab-created stones retain 60–75% potency. Synthetic/imitation stones have zero astrological value. If budget is tight, buy a smaller natural gem rather than a large synthetic. A 2-carat natural emerald works better than a 10-carat fake. Alternatively, start with a treated gem, and upgrade to natural when finances allow.

**Q: Can I remove a gemstone after wearing it for a while?**

A: Yes, safely. Removing a gem doesn't cause harm; it simply stops that planetary amplification. You can remove it if: (1) The dasha or beneficial transit ends, (2) You want to test if the gem is helping, (3) You need a break from its intensity. Temporary removal (days or weeks) doesn't hurt. Long-term removal after months of wearing might feel like losing support. If the gem is serving you, keep wearing indefinitely.

**Q: What's the difference between planetary gems and regular gemstone jewelry?**

A: Regular jewelry prioritizes beauty; planetary gems prioritize astrological potency. A beautiful emerald might be heavily treated, low quality, or wrong weight—making it useless astrologically. A planetary emerald is natural, high-clarity, proper weight, set in the prescribed metal, and worn on the prescribed finger with ritual. Never assume beautiful jewelry has planetary value; authenticate astrologically, not just aesthetically.

**Q: Should I remove gemstones during sleep, inauspicious times, or rituals?**

A: Yes, for optimal results. Remove during: (1) Sleep—your body needs break from continuous vibration, (2) Bathing—water weakens gems, (3) Inauspicious periods (Rahu Kaal, eclipses, Amavasya), (4) Religious fasting (Ekadashi), (5) Family funerals or inauspicious events. Resume wearing when the period passes. This on/off pattern actually strengthens the gem's effect by preventing habituation.
