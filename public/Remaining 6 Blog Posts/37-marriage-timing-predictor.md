## What is a Muhurat?

**Muhurat** wo "Golden Time Window" hai jab planets aur nakshatras ki position sabse favorable hoti hai. Is waqt universe ki energy aapke kaam ko support karti hai.

![Types of Muhurat - Marriage Griha Pravesh Business Child Birth icons](/images/post14-chart-types.png)

Muhurat kyun zaroori hai?
*   **Remove Obstacles:** Negative energies (jaise Rahu Kaal) ko avoid karne ke liye.
*   **Maximize Success:** Planetary alignment ka fayda uthane ke liye.
*   **Peace of Mind:** Ye psychological confidence deta hai ki humne sahi shuruat ki hai.

## Types of Muhurats We Calculate

Har kaam ke liye alag muhurat hota hai. Jo waqt shaadi ke liye acha hai, wo zaroori nahi ki business ke liye bhi acha ho.

![Find Muhurat - event type Panchang Rahu Kaal avoid transits check](/images/post14-process.png)

1.  **Vivah Muhurat (Marriage):** Sabse important. Ismein Kundli milan ke saath-saath sahi Lagna aur Nakshatra dekha jata hai.
2.  **Griha Pravesh (House Warming):** Naye ghar mein sukh-shanti ke liye. Vastu Shanti ke liye best time.
3.  **Vehicle Purchase:** Taaki gadi lambe samay tak chale aur accidents se bachi rahe.
4.  **Business Launch:** Profit aur growth ke liye 'Labh' ya 'Amrit' Choghadiya best hota hai.
5.  **Namkaran (Naming Ceremony):** Bachhe ke bright future ke liye.

## Times to AVOID: The Danger Zones

Muhurat dhoondne ka matlab sirf acha waqt dhoondna nahi, balki **Bura Waqt Avoid** karna bhi hai. In times par koi naya kaam shuru na karein:

![Times to AVOID - Rahu Kaal Yamaganda eclipses Amavasya warnings](/images/post14-compatibility.png)

*   **Rahu Kaal:** Har din ka wo 1.5 ghanta jab Rahu rule karta hai. Ismein shuru kiya kaam aksar fail hota hai.
*   **Yamaganda:** Ismein kaam karne se loss ya death-like suffering ho sakti hai.
*   **Panchak:** Kuch specific nakshatras ka group jismein shubh karya varjit (prohibited) hote hain.
*   **Amavasya (No Moon):** Is din negative energies high hoti hain, isliye shubh kaam avoid karein.

## Choghadiya: Quick Daily Guide

Agar aapke paas detail mein muhurat dekhne ka time nahi hai, toh **Choghadiya** best quick guide hai. Ye din ko 7 hisson mein baant-ta hai:

| Choghadiya | Meaning | Good for... |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Amrit** | Best (Nectar) | All auspicious works, Puja, Marriage. |
| **Shubh** | Good (Auspicious) | Marriage, Religious activities. |
| **Labh** | Gain (Benefit) | Business, Education, Travel, Starting new job. |
| **Char** | Neutral (Moving) | Travel, Vehicle purchase. |
| **Rog** | Bad (Disease) | Avoid new work. Can visit doctor/hospital. |
| **Kaal** | Bad (Death/Loss) | Strictly Avoid everything. |
| **Udveg** | Bad (Anxiety) | Avoid investments and government work. |

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: Kya aaj ka din naya business shuru karne ke liye acha hai?**
**A:** Iske liye aapko aaj ka **'Labh'** ya **'Amrit'** Choghadiya check karna chahiye aur dekhna chahiye ki us waqt Rahu Kaal toh nahi hai.

**Q2: Rahu Kaal kaise check karein?**
**A:** Rahu Kaal ka time har din aur har shehar (city) ke hisaab se badalta rehta hai. Hamara tool aapki location ke hisaab se exact Rahu Kaal batata hai.

**Q3: Abhijit Muhurat kya hai?**
**A:** Ye din ka wo special time hai (usually around 11:45 AM - 12:30 PM) jo Lord Vishnu ko priya hai. Ismein aap bina Kundli dekhe bhi shubh kaam kar sakte hain (Wednesday ko chhodkar).

**Q4: Kya Griha Pravesh sham ko kar sakte hain?**
**A:** Nahi, Griha Pravesh hamesha **Suraj dhalne se pehle** (daytime mein) karna chahiye. Raat ka pravesh shubh nahi mana jata.

**Q5: Mere paas muhurat wait karne ka time nahi hai, kya karun?**
**A:** Agar emergency ho, toh 'Hora' check karein ya phir Ganpati Bappa ka naam lekar aur thoda gud (jaggery) khakar kaam shuru karein.
